It's nice to meet you.

As a child of the 80’s, the Memphis Design-inspired aesthetics of Pee-Wee Herman, Nickelodeon, Trapper Keepers and Lisa Frank really spoke to me.

As an adult, I appreciate whenever I can inject bright colors, bold shapes and offbeat humor into everyday tasks, whether it's getting dressed for work, hosting a social event or keeping track of personal finances.

As a designer, I like to put the FUN back into FUNCTIONAL. Who says being an adult has to be so boring?

Working in web design and digital marketing allows me to exercise my creativity, while utilizing my high level of empathy and keen observation skills.

I’m inspired by this quote from one of my design heroes,

“When I was young, all we ever heard about was
functionalism, functionalism, functionalism.
It’s not enough. Design should also be sensual and exciting.”
-Ettore Sottsass
