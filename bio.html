It's nice to meet you.

My name is Katy and I'm a designer. While I am trained in the design and development of user-friendly web applications, I was born with the creativity, empathy and eye for design that are essential to being successful in this field.

I have spent several years working in entertainment industry marketing and market research.

In these fast-paced, deadline driven environments, I learned how to juggle many tasks at once without sacrificing the quality of my output. I honed my communications skills and tested my adaptability through international collaborations and partnerships. Most importantly, I never backed down from any challenge and solidified my passion for design.

Now that I am back in my hometown of Cleveland, I am excited to begin the next chapter of my professional journey with the unique perspective I gained from working in Los Angeles.

Let the adventure continue!
